SPACE App - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to SPACE App, a healthcare application designed to display standardised clinical pathways for providing medical guidelines developed at the Royal Surrey NHS Foundation Trust. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SPACE App is designed with your privacy in mind and does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Usage data
  • Device information
  • Cookies or tracking information
  • Web analytics data

2. Use of the Application

SPACE App serves as a resource for Royal Surrey NHS Foundation Trust healthcare professionals by providing:

  • Access to medical guidelines.
  • Information that can be viewed and read directly within the app without requiring any personal data input or account creation.
  • Traverse through pathways with simple Yes/No questions and view related documents.

3. No Data Sharing

Since SPACE App does not collect any personal information, we do not share any data with third parties. There is no sharing, selling, or leasing of user data because no such data is collected.

4. Security

Although SPACE App does not collect personal information, we are committed to maintaining a secure environment for the information provided within the app. We implement standard security measures to protect the app from unauthorized access and ensure the accuracy and integrity of the information displayed.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

6. Conclusion

Your privacy is our priority. By using SPACE App, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to this Privacy Policy. We are dedicated to providing you with a secure and private experience while delivering valuable healthcare information.
